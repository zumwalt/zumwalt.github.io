Preboarding

A Ruby on Rails app for facilitating moodboard excercises.

Simple Focus often does moodboard excercises with clients at the beginning of projects. It serves as a way to create a visual vocabulary when they speak about their brand. What does "clean" really mean when it comes to design?

As a way to make contributing easier for remote stakeholders, I built a Rails app that allows multiple stakeholders to give input on a moodboard. Each user can click to place a "sticker" containing their initials and choose a color based on their opinion. The stickers create conversation points that allow us to dig deeper into their perception of their brand.
