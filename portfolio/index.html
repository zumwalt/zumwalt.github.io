Preboarding

A Ruby on Rails app for facilitating moodboard excercises.

My team at Simple Focus often does moodboard excercises with clients at the beginning of projects. It serves as a way to create a visual vocabulary when they speak about their brand. What does "clean" actually mean when it comes to design?

As a way to make contributing easier for remote stakeholders, I built a Rails app that allows multiple users to give input on a moodboard at the same time. Each user can click to place a sticker containing their initials and a color that reflects their opinion. The stickers create conversation points that allow us to dig deeper into each stakeholder's perception of their brand. We, in turn, have a clearer understanding of the brand and their expectations before we get too far into the design process.

Boats.com

A redesign and strategic rethinking of an underdeveloped brand.

Boats.com is a boating news, resources, and classifieds site. My team at Simple Focus was engaged to not only redesign it's UI, but to also research it's users and explore other strategies for the site as a whole. Using information gathered from user feedback, we shifted the focus of Boats.com from classifieds to editorial, highlighting the large amount of content their team was already producing. I was responsible for design and front-end prototyping. This project is still in development with Boats.com's team.
